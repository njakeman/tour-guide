# Out of the Village

The path leaves Glynde beside the forge and climbs steadily west. Within ten minutes the valley opens behind you — the Ouse threading south toward Lewes, Firle Beacon rising beyond.

## Look Out For

- The dry coombe on your left, cut by meltwater at the end of the last Ice Age
- Skylarks holding territory over the summit grassland
- The first glimpse of the rampart as a green step against the skyline

> "Caburn stands apart from the Downs the way a keep stands apart from its bailey — joined, but deliberately alone."
